Constructs a projectile with movement parameters and collision filtering.

The collision layer is selected based on isFromPlayer and the mask is configured to collide with the appropriate opposing entities plus environment.

Parameters
spawnPointInitial world position.
directionDirection of travel (may be normalized by derived types).
speedUnits per second.
damageDamage dealt on hit.
isFromPlayerTrue when spawned by the player.
